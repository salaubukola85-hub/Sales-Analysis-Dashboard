# Sales and Customer Growth Easy Technologies Internship January 2026
Interactive Excel Sales Dashboard analyzing Sales, Customer, and Product data

## Project Overview
This project is an **interactive Sales Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales, customer, and product data for 2023–2024. The dashboard provides **actionable insights** to support data-driven decision-making, helping management understand business performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional opportunities.  

The goal of the project is to enable **quick, clear, and interactive analysis** of key performance indicators (KPIs) to optimize sales, customer engagement, and strategic planning.

---

## Business Questions
The dashboard answers the following key business questions:  
1. How much revenue did the business generate overall this year?  
2. How many orders were placed, and what is the average order value (AOV)?  
3. Which months had the highest and lowest sales, and are there seasonal trends?  
4. Which customers generate the most revenue and/or place the most orders?  
5. What is the revenue distribution across different age groups and genders?  
6. Which product categories or individual products perform best and worst?  
7. Which regions contribute the most and least to revenue and customer base?  
8. Where are the opportunities for growth, and which areas or products need improvement?  
9. How can management make data-driven decisions to improve sales, customer retention, and profitability?  

---

## Problem Statement
Management struggles to quickly assess **sales performance**, identify growth opportunities, and track customer behavior due to fragmented data. There is a lack of **interactive, actionable insights**, which slows decision-making and prevents effective strategic planning.  

The Sales Dashboard project addresses these challenges by **centralizing data**, highlighting trends, and providing clear KPIs to guide business decisions.

---

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard is an **interactive Excel tool** built with pivot tables, calculations, and slicers to allow users to explore data by:  
- **Month and Year**  
- **Region** (West, East, South, North)  
- **Product Category** (Sports, Clothing, etc.)  
- **Customer Segment** (Age, Gender)  

Features include:  
- KPI cards summarizing **Total Sales, Orders, AOV, Quantity Sold, Total Customers, and Average Age**  
- Interactive charts for **monthly trends, product/category performance, customer behavior, and regional analysis**  
- Slicers to filter and analyze **specific dimensions** of the data

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing on **high-value age groups (35–65 years)**, especially female customers.  
- Implement **loyalty programs** for top customers like James Adams and Kathleen Kelly.  
- Expand marketing and sales presence in the **North region** to capture untapped potential.  
- Invest in **high-performing product categories** (Sports) and review strategy for **underperforming categories** (Clothing).  
- Align **promotions and inventory planning** with peak months (January, May, November) and create targeted campaigns during low months (June, July).  
- Leverage the dashboard for **real-time KPI monitoring, forecasting, and data-driven decision-making**.
